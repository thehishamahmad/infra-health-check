What We Assess

The Infrastructure Health Check Assessment provides a high-level review of your current environment and helps identify infrastructure weaknesses, operational gaps, and future growth requirements before they impact business operations.

The assessment focuses on four areas that typically have the greatest effect on business continuity, service availability, storage sufficiency, and network performance.

Current Infrastructure

Know the scale and readiness of your environment

We review the current infrastructure landscape to understand the number of servers, virtual machines, and critical workloads currently supporting the business.

  • Server and VM footprint
  • Single point of failure
  • Scalability and capacity planning
  • Current architecture readiness

Availability & DR

Know your recovery gap

We assess whether the environment is prepared to recover from an outage, infrastructure failure, or major incident.

  • Backup frequency and retention
  • Recovery Time Objective (RTO)
  • Recovery Point Objective (RPO)
  • Disaster Recovery readiness

Storage & Growth

Know your data readiness

We review current storage usage and forecast future data growth to determine whether the environment can support future business demand.

  • Current storage consumption
  • Data growth trend
  • Archive and retention strategy
  • Future storage requirements

Network

Know your network performance

We assess whether the current network is secure, resilient, and capable of supporting future growth and hybrid connectivity.

  • Public vs private access
  • Load balancing readiness
  • Latency and performance
  • Hybrid connectivity and security

Why This Assessment Matters

Many organizations only discover infrastructure weaknesses after an outage, failed restore, storage shortage, or performance issue has already occurred. This assessment helps identify those gaps earlier and supports better planning.

Reduce Downtime Risk

Identify single points of failure and assess whether current infrastructure can continue supporting business operations during disruption.

Improve DR Readiness

Evaluate backup frequency, restore confidence, recovery expectations, and the level of Disaster Recovery preparedness across critical systems.

Plan Future Storage Growth

Review whether current storage capacity, retention planning, and archive strategy are sufficient to support future data growth.

Strengthen Network Resilience

Evaluate network exposure, performance limitations, and readiness for secure connectivity across branch, cloud, and hybrid environments.
